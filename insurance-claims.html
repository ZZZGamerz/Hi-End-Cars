Claim Process

1. Intimation: Call us immediately after the accident.

2. Pickup: We tow your car to our workshop.

3. Surveyor Visit: We coordinate the inspection.

4. Approval: Work starts once insurance approves.

5. Delivery: You pay only the deductible amount.
